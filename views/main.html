What's Kokus ?

Kokus is a generative world build by your use of technology. Have you ever ask yourself what your data must look like ? We have the answer. Kokus generate a cute world who grows each time something happen on your phone. Let trees, houses and mountains grow !

But we know you have some destructives impulses. So we had a physical button, which allow you to DESTROY THE WORLD !!!!

No seriously. Push that button, and your world will burn into ashes, and like the phoenix, reborn, so you can build a new one :)

See it live !

How it works ?

It's pretty simple. We watch for some events on your phone, like SMS receipt, battery changement, phone calls. Then littles elves build stuff in your world. Trees, houses, mountains.

And the destruction of your world, well. Push the button, and then the bomb inside your world will explode. Simple as f*ck.
